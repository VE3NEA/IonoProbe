Related Events

A single event on the Sun can cause a variety of ionospheric disturbances that affect HF propagation. The graphs below illustrate a typical sequence of events.





May 22, 2002 03:54 UTC:
A large filament eruption occurred on the Sun.

8 minutes later:
An x-ray burst arrived at the Earth and caused an SWF event.

14 hours later:
Energetic protons reached the ionosphere, a PCA event started.

31 hour later:
A severe geomagnetic storm commenced when a cloud of sollar matter reached the Earth.
