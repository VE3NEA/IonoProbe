Systray Icon

The icon in the System Tray is not just an indicator of the running IonoProbe. It serves as a gateway to all program's functions and also provides some storm information.

  • Click on the icon to open the IonoProbe's Main Window;
  • Right-click on it to open the Popup Menu
    • ;
  • Move your mouse over the icon to see the storm alert message as a mouse-over hint.
When a space storm is in progress, the icon turns from blue to red .
