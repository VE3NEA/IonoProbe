Affected areas

Our flagship product for radio amateurs, DX Atlas, includes a set dynamic ionospheric maps. A few DX Atlas screenshots are provided below to illlustrate the spatial coverage of the ionospheric affects discussed in this Help file.



Solar (SSN, SFI) and geomagnetic (Kp, Ap) activity affects the F layer of the ionosphere over the whole globe:



F Layer critical frequency map




Solar activity affects the D and E ionospheric layers over the sunlit hemisphere. Shortwave fadeouts (see X-Ray Flux) also cover the sunlit hemisphere but affect only the D layer:




D Layer electron density map



Auroral activity affects the Auroral ovals around the geomagnetic poles. Polar Cap events (see Proton Flux) affect polar caps, the areas inside the Auroral ovals:
